THESIS

The core thesis

AI is replacing traditional search as the primary interface for software discovery, but LLMs are guessing when making recommendations[2]. Traditional review platforms (G2, Capterra) are unverified, static, and unreadable for AI systems[2]. VerifiedLayer is the API-first trust infrastructure that powers how LLMs cite, evaluate, and recommend software[2].

VerifiedLayer replaces unstructured, manipulated review sites with an API-driven, machine-readable intelligence layer[2].
