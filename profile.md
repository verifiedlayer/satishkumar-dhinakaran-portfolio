## 🚀 What I'm Building: VerifiedLayer.ai

AI platforms process over **$1.4T in global software purchasing decisions**, yet **60%+ of AI search citations are hallucinated or wrong**. Modern Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) allows low-quality blog posts and "AI memory poisoning" plugins to manipulate AI recommendations effortlessly.

**VerifiedLayer** replaces unstructured, manipulated review sites with an API-driven, machine-readable intelligence layer.

* **Signal Engine:** Aggregates real-time user sentiment, technical credibility metrics, and cryptographic reputation signals.
* **Machine-Readable Structure:** Outputs verified trust scores via a Unified API designed for direct ingestion by LLMs (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude).
* **The Flywheel:** Every AI citation citing VerifiedLayer creates a free, organic acquisition channel that drives software buyers to our ecosystem, accelerating dataset depth and platform authority.
